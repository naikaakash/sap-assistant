# OrderPulse Brand Guidelines

OrderPulse is an enterprise-grade procurement action workbench designed for buyers and planners working on active supplier follow-up. 

## 1. Brand Identity & Positioning
- **Primary Name**: OrderPulse
- **Tagline**: PO follow-up, prioritized.
- **Tone**: Professional, precise, action-oriented, trustworthy, and enterprise-friendly.

## 2. Color Palette
OrderPulse matches clean B2B SaaS styles using a Royal Blue accent theme:
- **Primary Accent / Brand Color**: Royal Blue (`#2563eb`)
- **Secondary Accent**: Light Blue (`#3b82f6` / `#60a5fa`)
- **Primary Text**: Dark Slate (`#0f172a` / `#1e293b`)
- **Secondary Text**: Medium Slate (`#475569`)
- **Backgrounds**: Slate Gray (`#f8fafc`) and Clean White (`#ffffff`)

## 3. Typography
- **Headings & Logo**: Plus Jakarta Sans
- **Body & Tables**: System Font Stack (-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", Roboto)

## 4. Logo Assets
The logo combines a folded purchase order sheet outline, a status signal pulse line, and an active signal dot indicating operational monitoring.
- **Horizontal Logo (Light Background)**: `/brand/orderpulse-logo-horizontal-dark.svg`
- **Horizontal Logo (Dark Sidebar)**: `/brand/orderpulse-logo-horizontal.svg`
- **Square Mark (Favicon / Collapsed state)**: `/brand/orderpulse-logo-icon.svg`

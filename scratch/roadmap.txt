Procurement Buyer/Planner Action Workbench Roadmap
Product VisionA Buyer/Planner Action Workbench that helps procurement and supply chain teams identify problems, prioritize actions, collaborate with suppliers, and eventually automate follow-up activities.Core Philosophy:Visibility first → Actionability second → Automation third
Phase 1 – Visibility Foundation
1A – Executive Overview: KPI dashboard, filters, search-driven PO details.
1B – Overdue PO Workbench: Buyer worklist for overdue POs.
1C – Supplier Acknowledgement Workbench: Supplier response visibility.
1D – Part Availability Workbench: Inventory and shortage visibility.
1E – Control Tower Consolidation: Unified navigation and visibility platform.
Phase 2 – Guided Actions
2A – Action Recommendations: Suggest buyer actions.
2B – Prioritization Engine: Risk and impact scoring.
2C – Supplier Performance Analytics: Supplier KPIs and trends.
2D – Exception Analytics: Procurement exception insights.
2E – Buyer Productivity Workbench: Action tracking and productivity.
Phase 3 – AI Assistance
3A – Procurement Copilot: AI assistant for procurement questions.
3B – Root Cause Analysis: AI explanations of issues.
3C – Supplier Intelligence: AI supplier summaries.
3D – Predictive Risk: Delay and shortage predictions.
3E – Executive AI Insights: Executive summaries and reporting.
Phase 4 – Workflow Automation
4A – Supplier Reminder Agent: Human-approved reminders.
4B – Acknowledgement Follow-Up Agent: Tracks and follows up.
4C – Escalation Agent: Escalation workflows.
4D – Planner Collaboration Agent: Buyer-planner coordination.
4E – Multi-Agent Workflow: Agents working together.
Phase 5 – Autonomous Procurement Agents
5A – Autonomous Monitoring: 24x7 monitoring.
5B – Autonomous Supplier Engagement: Automated supplier contact.
5C – Autonomous Risk Detection: Proactive risk identification.
5D – Procurement Decision Support: Recommendations and alternatives.
5E – Procurement Agent Platform: Full multi-agent procurement platform.
Current MVP
PO Overdue Agent, Supplier Acknowledgement Agent, and Part Availability Agent surfaced through a Buyer/Planner Action Workbench.
